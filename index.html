Brief Bio

Yingqian Wang received his B.E. degree from Shandong University in 2016, and his M.E. degree from NUDT in 2018. He is currently working towards his Ph.D. degree with the College of Electronic Science and Technology, NUDT. His research interests mainly focus on low-level vision, particularly on light field imaging and image super-resolution. Mr. Wang won the China National Scholarship for three times from 2013 to 2015, and won the First Prize in the Final of the Chinese Mathematics Competitions in 2015.
